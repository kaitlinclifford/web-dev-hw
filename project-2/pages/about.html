About Me

My name is Kaitlin Clifford, and I'm a Media Arts student at the University of Montana. I'm still new to the whole Media Arts thing, but I'm really enjoying it so far. Every day is exciting because I get to create different projects.

This site is a project for my Web Design class. I chose to do a recipe on how to make rice crispy treats because everyone should know how to make these delicious treats. I'm sure most people have made them before, but if not, I wanted to make a place where people could make them easily.

On this page, you will see that I've included the address for a walmart where you can find all of the ingredients to make, these treats at home, as well as a song that can get you fired up while you make these treats. I do have to warn you that the song I have picked for you is in Korean, however, even if you can't understand it, that doesn't mean this song isn't popping!
