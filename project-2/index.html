Easy Rice Crispy Treats

By: Kaitlin Clifford

This Instructable is for anyone looking to create easy rice crispy treats, in very little time

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • One 10 1/2 oz. bag JET-PUFFED miniature marshmallows
  • 6 cups KELLOGG'S RICE KRISPIES cereal

Instructions

  • STEP 1: Melt butter in LARGE saucepan, on LOW heat.
  • STEP 2: Add marshmallows; cook until marshmallows are COMPLETELY melted, and the mixture is WELL blended, Stirring Constantly.
  • STEP 3: Remove from heat.
  • STEP 4: Add cereal; MIX WELL.
  • STEP 5: Press onto bottom of 13x9-inch pan sprayed with cooking spray. Cool completely.
